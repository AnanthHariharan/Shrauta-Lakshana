COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS: TYPE-TOKEN vs TOKEN-ONLY ANALYSIS
======================================================================

METHODOLOGY COMPARISON
--------------------------------------------------
Traditional Token Analysis:
• Counts total occurrences of linguistic patterns
• Measures frequency and productivity
• Focuses on usage patterns

Type-Token Analysis:
• Distinguishes unique forms (types) from total occurrences (tokens)
• Measures morphological diversity and vocabulary richness
• Reveals productive vs. fossilized patterns

KEY INSIGHTS FROM TYPE-TOKEN ANALYSIS
--------------------------------------------------

  Early Vedic: 0.3116 (range: 0.2494-0.4429)
  Late Vedic: 0.3106 (range: 0.2206-0.4346)

  long compounds: TTR = 0.844
  instrumental archaic: TTR = 0.351
  philosophical terms: TTR = 0.303
  deity names: TTR = 0.240
  ritual terms: TTR = 0.206

IMPLICATIONS FOR VEDIC SANSKRIT STUDIES
--------------------------------------------------
• Type-token ratios reveal morphological complexity changes
• High TTR indicates productive, creative language use
• Low TTR suggests formulaic or ritualized language
• Diachronic TTR changes track linguistic evolution
• Hapax legomena patterns validate text authenticity
